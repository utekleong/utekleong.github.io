Main

Leong Utek

Broadly, I am interested in advancing healthcare research in Singapore through the study of individual differences. I am currently researching the cognitive-affective vulnerabilities to internalising psychopathology from a network perspective. My specific research interests include:

  • Hierarchical and network approaches to psychopathology
  • History of psychology/psychiatry
  • Individual differences in treatment adherence and its associated processes

To supplement my training as a psychological researcher, I have also been pursuing collaboration with researchers from the NUS Saw Swee Hock School of Public Health and Duke-NUS Medical School to broaden my exposure to research in the health domain.
